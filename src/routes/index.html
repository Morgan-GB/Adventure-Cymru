Who are we?

Adventure Cymru is Wales' favourite outdoor centre. We offer a range of activities, like kite surfing, kayaking and more.

Recently, Adventure Cymru has won a grant! This has allowed us to run residential courses and allow our visitors to experience an even wider range of activities when they visit over several days! These multi-day visits help build confidence and get people moving.

By far, our customer favourite activity is kitesurfing! But, if that doesn't tickle your fancy, you can check out the rest of our activities here.
