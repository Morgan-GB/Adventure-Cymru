Kitesurfing

What is Kitesurfing?

Kitesurfing is a wind-powered water surface sport where you stand on a board and usse a kite to move across the surface of the water. The kite makes use of wind to propel you forward.

Skills & attributes for kitesurfing

  • Relatively good fitness (especially in the core)
  • Swimming ability
  • Good self-confidence & the ability to try new things
  • The ability to stay calm

Learning to Kitesurf

When learning to Kitesurf with Adventure Cymru, you will go through 3 steps:

  1. Flying a kite on land (launching, landing & kite control)
  2. Flying a kite in the water (body dragging, control & re-launching)
  3. Standing on & riding on a board in the water.

These steps will also give you experience in:

  • Assessing an area to make sure it is safe to kite
  • Understanding wind direction
  • Understanding tides, currents & hazards
  • Choosing the right equipment
  • Setting-up, tuning & packing away your equipment
  • Safety procedures
  • What to do in an emergency
  • Board-starts (standing up on the board)
  • Continuous riding
  • Speed control
